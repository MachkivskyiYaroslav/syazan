About

The owl family is in trouble. An evil hunter kidnapped little owls in order to do his evil things. Help the mother owl to find her children and defeat the evil hunter. Puzzle words, earn points, bypass obstacles on the way to justice.

Fascinating gameplay will plunge you into a fabulous world where the Owl climb a tree. On the way she waits a lot of interesting situations. Kabkans, mined branches, broken branches - all this will force you to think carefully about your way.
